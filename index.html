About

Dr. Hamza Ajeena is a dedicated veterinarian with a passion for animal health and welfare. He earned his Doctor of Veterinary Medicine (DVM) degree from the University of Baghdad in 2002. Following his graduation, Dr. Ajeena gained valuable experience working in various veterinary settings, including clinics and animal hospitals, where he honed his skills in diagnosing and treating a wide range of animal health issues.

In 2014, Dr. Ajeena relocated to the United States to further his career. He has since been involved in veterinary practices in the Chicago area, focusing on providing high-quality care to pets and supporting their owners with education and resources. Dr. Ajeena is known for his compassionate approach and commitment to staying current with advancements in veterinary medicine.
