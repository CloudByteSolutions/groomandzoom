About Us

At Groom and Zoom, we're Chicago's premier mobile dog grooming service, bringing professional pet care directly to your doorstep. Founded by Hamza Ajeena, a Doctor of Veterinary Medicine and Emergency Veterinarian in Lincoln Park, and Adam Alaraj, a passionate dog owner, we combine veterinary expertise with exceptional grooming services.

Our state-of-the-art mobile grooming vans and certified professional groomers provide stress-free, convenient grooming services throughout Chicago. We prioritize your pet's health, safety, and comfort while delivering salon-quality results in the convenience of your home.

Why Choose Groom and Zoom?

  • Veterinarian-owned and operated
  • Certified professional groomers
  • State-of-the-art mobile grooming vans
  • Stress-free home environment
  • Convenient scheduling
  • Serving all Chicago neighborhoods

Work with Us

At Groom and Zoom, we're revolutionizing pet care by bringing top-notch grooming services directly to our clients' doors. Based in the heart of Chicago, we pride ourselves on providing a stress-free, convenient, and luxurious grooming experience for pets and their owners. Our state-of-the-art mobile grooming vans and passionate team make us the go-to choice for pet parents across the city.

We're looking for dedicated, skilled, and compassionate Mobile Dog Groomers to join our growing team. If you love working with animals and are passionate about making pets look and feel their best, we'd love to hear from you!

Responsibilities

  • Perform grooming services including bathing, brushing, trimming, and styling for dogs of all breeds and sizes.
  • Operate and maintain a clean, safe, and organized mobile grooming van.
  • Provide exceptional customer service, ensuring a positive experience for both pets and their owners.
  • Follow grooming instructions and preferences from pet owners.
  • Adhere to all safety and sanitation protocols.

Requirements

  • Proven experience as a professional dog groomer (certification is a plus).
  • Knowledge of different dog breeds, coat types, and grooming techniques.
  • Ability to handle dogs of all sizes and temperaments with patience and care.
  • Strong communication and customer service skills.
  • Reliable transportation to and from the grooming van's base location.
  • Flexibility to work weekdays, weekends, and occasional evenings.
  • Must be physically able to lift and handle dogs up to 50 lbs.

Why Join Us?

  • Competitive pay and tips.
  • Flexible scheduling.
  • Work independently in a state-of-the-art mobile grooming van.
  • Be part of a supportive and passionate team.
  • Make a difference in the lives of pets and their owners every day!
